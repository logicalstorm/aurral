---
title: History
description: Track Lidarr requests, slskd downloads, and Aurral playlist activity.
---

History replaces the older Requests and Downloads views with one timeline.

![Aurral history timeline](../../../assets/screenshots/history.webp)

Filter by source:

- **Lidarr** — artist and album requests, queue progress, and import status
- **slskd** — Soulseek download activity for flows and playlists
- **Aurral** — playlist generation and download jobs

Use History when you want a single place to see what is processing, what failed, and what finished.

When an album request fails in Lidarr, you can retry the search from History when the entry supports it.
