Exportify ➜ Aurral

  1. Log in to Exportify.net with your Spotify account.
  2. Click Export next to the playlist you want to bring to Aurral.
  3. Drop the downloaded .csv file into the box below and click Convert.
  4. Open your Aurral web UI, go to the Playlist Import modal, and upload the generated JSON!
