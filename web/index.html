One interface from recommendation to playback.

We stand by our recommendation engine. See what fits your library, then download and listen without reinventing the rest of your stack.

  • See library state before you request.
  • Track Lidarr requests, slskd downloads, and playlist jobs in History.
  • Run weekly flows and static playlists side by side.
  • Connect Spotify playlists in Aurral, or use the CSV converter as a fallback. See the import guide.
